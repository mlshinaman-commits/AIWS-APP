    04 / AI Fraud Shield™

    Eleven layers between fraud
    and your payroll.

    Point solutions check a document or scan a face. Fraud Shield correlates identity, media, credentials, and behavior across the entire hiring journey — because sophisticated fraud only breaks when every layer talks to every other layer.

    Layer 01

    Identity Verification

    Government-ID authentication with biometric liveness — confirmed at application, interview, and day one, so the person never changes between steps.

    Layer 02

    Deepfake Detection

    Real-time video and audio analysis during interviews: frame-level artifacts, micro-acoustic signatures, and voice-face correspondence checks.

    Layer 03

    Resume Validation

    Cross-references claims against public records and digital footprint; flags AI-fabricated histories and impossible timelines.

    Layer 04

    Employment Verification

    Confirms employers, titles, and dates through payroll-network and registry data — not just the references the candidate chose.

    Layer 05

    Credential Verification

    Degrees, licenses, and certifications validated directly with issuing institutions and accreditation registries.

    Layer 06

    Behavioral Risk Detection

    Response latency, answer provenance, and assistance patterns that indicate scripted or AI-coached interviews.

    Layer 07

    Interview Fraud Detection

    Catches teleprompting, off-screen coaching, and answer feeding the moment it happens — with reviewable evidence clips.

    Layer 08

    Offshore Proxy Detection

    Network, device, and timezone forensics that expose stand-ins interviewing — or working — on someone else's behalf.

    Layer 09

    Synthetic Identity Detection

    Graph analysis that spots assembled identities: real SSN, fabricated history, borrowed face — the profile that never existed.

    Layer 10

    Candidate Risk Scoring

    Every signal fuses into one explainable 0–100 trust score with evidence trails your legal team can stand behind.

    Layer 11

    Continuous Workforce Verification

    Verification doesn't end at the offer. Periodic re-checks confirm the person you hired is the person doing the work.

    Outcome

    Zero fraudulent hires

    How a verification runs
    STEP 1
    Capture signals

    Documents, biometrics, interview media, device and network telemetry collected with candidate consent at each stage.

    STEP 2
    Run the ensemble

    Specialized models — vision, audio, NLP, graph — analyze each signal class in parallel, in seconds.

    STEP 3
    Score & explain

    Signals fuse into a trust score with plain-language reasons and linked evidence for every flag.

    STEP 4
    Act in workflow

    Auto-advance, request re-verification, or route to your fraud review queue — inside your existing ATS.
